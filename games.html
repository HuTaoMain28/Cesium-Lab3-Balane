Devil May Cry 5

Devil May Cry 5 is a 2019 action-adventure game developed and published by Capcom. The game is the sixth installment overall and the fifth mainline installment in the Devil May Cry series. It takes place five years after Devil May Cry 4 and follows a trio of warriors with demonic powers, the returning Dante, Nero, and a new protagonist named V, as they attempt to stop the Demon King Urizen from destroying the human world. Across their journey in Red Grave City, the player can use these characters in different missions. Each of them has their own way of fighting and becoming stronger. As this happens, the mystery behind V is revealed, along with his connection with Urizen.

5 reasons why this game is my favorite:.

  • The game has great combat mechanics
  • The game has great and interesting story
  • The game has diverse and unique characters with different play styles
  • The game has only one ending, which makes it replayable and retry-able
  • The game has different difficulty levels, making it easy for new players to try out some mechanics
